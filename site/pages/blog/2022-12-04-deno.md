Lately I stumbled across another runtime for JavaScript called [Deno](https://deno.land/). Deno claims to be better than NodeJS in many regards (faster, more secure, easier), and has a much cooler website than NodeJS.

They also have a [Docker Image](https://hub.docker.com/r/denoland/deno) available, so expect a size and resource comparison of NodeJS vs. Deno soon on my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCkeCpLtQixK4IiOq9ukonyQ) 😉
